Our Story

Founded in 2025, Semakaleng Chesanyama celebrates South African culinary heritage with an authentic braai experience that brings communities together. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us recognition as a top emerging restaurant.

Our Values

At Semakaleng Chisanyama, we believe in authenticity, community, and quality. These principles guide everything we do, from our kitchen to your table.

Meet the Team

BJANG MASONIA JOSEPH

As the founder of Semakaleng Chisanyama, BJANG MASONIA JOSEPH created a vibrant social hub for great food and lively atmosphere. His contributions have made shisa nyama a beloved part of South African culture.

MOKOENA SEMAKALENG SELDAH

Chef MOKOENA SEMAKALENG SELDAH is renowned for his expertise in traditional South African cuisine. A judge on MasterChef South Africa, Chef SEMAKALENG is dedicated to celebrating and elevating South African food culture.
